    Site Purpose

    The purpose of this website is to promote trade and investment among the city companies in different sectors, as well as to help them face each of the challenges and opportunities that globalization offers. To achieve this purpose,the Chamber of Commerce has a large group of members, which are companies from all the state that carry out operations, as well as business organizations, including the Chambers of Commerce of the different neighborhoods .
