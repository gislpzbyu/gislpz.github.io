Target Audience

Description

The target audience of this site is for people who are interested in knowing what the weather will be like every day because their daily activities depend on that, some trip, or just to know how to choose the outfit effect to leave home, some walk or work. Since the site is quite effective, no matter how old it is, it can be from a teenager to an old man who visits this page.

Personas

Persona 1

She is Maria, is 35 years old, an engineer in sustainable development, has 2 children, is married, and spends most of her time abroad as her income depends on it. She is interested to know how she will be every day in terms of the weather to ensure that she can carry out her activities without any difficulty, or unforeseen in the day.

Persona 2

He is Carlos, entrepreneur, cattle and ranch owner. He is 55 years old, has 3 children, is married. He spends most of his time on the ranch, plantings and cattle as he is engaged in the purchase and sale of the aforementioned. He is always being informed of how the weather situations will be within the week to be able to plan your his activities.

Scenarios

  • How should I dress the kids today?
  • Should I wear a coat in case it starts raining later?
  • How will the field be to coach football in the afternoon?
  • Should I plant fruits and vegetables this week?
  • What kind of shoes can I wear?
