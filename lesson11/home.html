Visit Idaho! A paradise for lovers of emotions throughout the year.

Idaho has more miles of bounded land than anywhere else in the continental United States. These natural areas, forest forests and local and state parks offer easy access to spectacular waterfalls, distinguished streams, ancient lava flows, amazing sand dunes, rivers without human intervention, crystal clear lakes and towering mountains. Cities and towns offer the comforts of home with accommodations, restaurants, food stores, gas stations, and serve as a natural base camp for outdoor recreation. Take the road less traveled on any of Idaho's 31 scenic unexplored trails while learning about the state's history, geology, and outdoor recreational opportunities. Idaho's highways and trails showcase the diverse beauty of the state and propel you on unforgettable adventures.
