Average weather in Preston Idaho, United States

In Preston, the summers are hot and mostly clear, the winters are freezing and partly cloudy, and it is dry year-round. During the course of the year, the temperature generally ranges from -9 ° C to 31 ° C and rarely drops below -17 ° C or rises above 34 ° C.
Based on the tourism score, the best time of year to visit Preston for hot weather activities is late June through mid August.
The hot season lasts for 3.1 months, from June 11 to September 16, and the average daily maximum temperature is over 25 ° C. The hottest day of the year is July 27, with an average maximum temperature of 31 ° C and an average minimum temperature of 12 ° C.
The cold season lasts for 3.1 months, from November 23 to February 27, and the average daily maximum temperature is less than 5 ° C. The coldest day of the year is January 7, with an average minimum temperature of -9 ° C and an average maximum of -1 ° C.
