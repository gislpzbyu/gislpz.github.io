Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide updates to every moment of the weather in all cities of the world in real time by providing temperature, humidity, wind, and forecasts for the whole week. Likewise, there will be news related to the topic, informative articles, and suggestions of places and / or activities to visit during this time and according to the weather.
